About LogiCore

🧠

Your Personal Academic Advisor,
Powered by Artificial Intelligence

LogiCore is a smart computer program that acts like an experienced university supervisor. It listens to your skills, goals, and situation — then recommends the best final year project for you, and explains exactly why.

🎓
What is LogiCore?

LogiCore is a Final Year Project Advisory System built for undergraduate Computer Science students at Maseno University. Think of it as a knowledgeable friend who has supervised hundreds of student projects and knows exactly which one is right for you.

🤔
How Does It Think?

The system uses a type of Artificial Intelligence called an Expert System. It stores knowledge from experienced supervisors in the form of rules, then applies those rules to your personal profile to arrive at a recommendation — just like a human expert would.

📋
What Does It Consider?

LogiCore looks at your academic GPA, the programming skills you have, your career goals after graduation, how many hours per week you can dedicate to the project, and whether you are working solo or in a group.

💡
What Do You Get?

You receive a ranked list of up to 6 project topics tailored specifically to you, each with a compatibility score, the tools you would use, an explanation of why it was recommended, and honest warnings about any challenges you might face.

"Many students choose final year projects based on trends or peer pressure rather than informed reasoning — leading to incomplete systems, poor outcomes, and wasted effort. LogiCore was built to fix that."

— LogiCore Design Philosophy, Maseno University 2026
The Problem
What students face today
  • 😟 Difficulty choosing a project that matches their skill level
  • ⏰ Supervisors too busy to give personalized guidance to every student
  • ❌ Students pick projects that are too complex or too simple
  • 🎯 Projects do not align with future career goals
  • 📉 High rates of incomplete or poorly executed projects
The Solution
What LogiCore provides
  • ✅ Instant, personalized project recommendations anytime
  • 🧭 Guidance based on your actual skills and GPA — not guesswork
  • 🎯 Projects matched to your specific career path
  • ⚖️ Complexity matched to what you can realistically achieve
  • 📖 Full explanation of every recommendation so you understand why

Using LogiCore in 4 Simple Steps

📝

Who Benefits from LogiCore?

The system was designed to serve multiple people in the academic community.

👨‍🎓
Students

Get honest, intelligent guidance on which project to choose without having to guess. Make an informed, confident decision backed by expert reasoning.

👩‍🏫
Lecturers & Supervisors

Reduce the time spent on basic project selection consultations. Students arrive better prepared and with more realistic project ideas.

🏛️
Universities

Improve overall project quality and completion rates. Better student outcomes enhance the institution's academic reputation.

🔬
Researchers

A real-world case study of applying AI Expert Systems to education — demonstrating the practical value of knowledge-based systems.
